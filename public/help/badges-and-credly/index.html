Help: Badges and Credly

Connecting to Credly.com and Awarding Badges:

  • Mouse uses a third-party site, Credly.com, to manage our Digital Badges.  Students who are over 13 must create a Credly account to be able to receive and share their badges. These badges can be shared publicly on social media sites, on college applications and on resumes.

  • Students become eligible for the Badge when they have submitted work for each project related to the badge, and after you have reviewed each of these projects.  

  • To view which projects are required for each Mouse Badge, click here.

  • Award a Badge: Once a student becomes eligible for a Badge, an area will appear beneath “Project Review” on your Group page. Click on the Badge, check the box to agree to the terms and click “Award Badge”.

  • This badge will then appear on their Mouse profile, and a public URL for this badge will be created that the student can share.
