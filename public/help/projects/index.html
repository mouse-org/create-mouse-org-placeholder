Help: Projects

Preparing to Facilitate a Project:

  • Projects that require facilitation are often tagged “With a Group” — meaning this project can not be done by a student on their own.

  • To facilitate a project on Mouse Create, first open it by clicking on the project from the Project Finder or within your Group’s Playlist.  

  • Before leading, read through the project’s Background.

  • In the steps menu to the right of the project, at the bottom, is the Background information about the project.  Here you will find a list of outcomes, materials, and instructions on how to prep the activity.  You will also find a link to the project’s standards alignment, debrief/reflection questions and any suggested prerequisite or follow-up projects.

  • We recommend going through each of the project’s steps on your own before leading with youth.

Working on a Project:

  • Students, or small groups of students, can either follow along step-by-step on their own devices or you could display the steps on a projector or smartboard for the whole group.  

  • Show your Work: On each step, there is a “Show your Work” area at the bottom.  This area often has required reflection questions, or a request to document their work with an image or text.  

  • Review and Submit: At the end of each project, students must review and submit their work to receive credit for having completed the project.  

  • Add Collaborators: If students are working in teams, or as a whole group, they can tag other users in your group as “collaborators” before submitting the project. This will give those users credit for having completed the project as well.

  • Finished projects will be submitted for review by the group educator- you can find these on your group page at the bottom, in an area called “Project Review”.  

  • Note: students in multiple groups need to make sure the correct group is active at the top of the page before starting a project.

Reviewing Projects:

  • After your group members have clicked finished their project by clicking “Submit” on the “Review and Submit” step, they will show up on your Group page under the Playlist- in the Project Review area.

Click on the project name to open a window that will show all of the submitted work.  If you are satisfied with their submission, check the “Mark Reviewed” box and then “Update”.
