Help: Groups and Playlists

Create a New Group

  1. After entering your Educator Code, click on “Create Group” in the lower right.
  2. Name your Group, check the terms, and create your first group!
  3. Your Group page will be empty until you add projects to your Playlist.

Add Projects to your Group’s Playlist:

  1. Click on “Projects” at the top of the page- you will not see this until you have created a Group.
  2. Browse all the Projects, or use the Project Finder tags on the left hand side of the page to filter projects according to their Course, Skill area, or Setting (time and size of group).
  3. When you find a project you’d like to add to your Group’s playlist, click “Add to Group”.
  4. You’ll be prompted to choose which Group you’d like to add the project to. Check your Group’s box and click “Add to Group.”
  5. If you’re working with multiple groups of learners, you can customize the projects for each group through this function.
  6. To review the Projects you’ve selected go to your Group’s page by clicking on your Group’s name at the top of the page.

Delete or Rearrange your Playlist: You can remove or re-order the projects in your playlist by clicking on the Manage button next to the word playlist.

Multiple Groups:

  1. If you are working with various classes or clubs, you can create multiple Groups by going to your Dashboard and following the same process you did before.
  2. You can switch between them with the “Switch Group” drop down menu at the top of the page (this will not appear if you only have one Group).
  3. To review a given Group’s Playlist, switch to that Group and click on the Group’s name at the top of the page.
